AUTI FOUNDATION

Auti Foundation is a non-profit initiative founded by Dilip Auti and Komal Auti with a vision to create meaningful and lasting change in society. The foundation works across key areas such as entrepreneurship, education, empowerment, and environmental sustainability to address real-world challenges and promote inclusive growth.

Driven by a strong sense of purpose, Auti Foundation is dedicated to serving communities, uplifting individuals, and nurturing self-reliance with dignity. Through impactful programs and initiatives, the foundation strives to inspire future generations to grow with values, responsibility, and a commitment to building a better society.

Core Focus Areas

1

Education & Skill Development

Providing scholarships, career guidance, and digital literacy programs to help children and youth build a strong future.
